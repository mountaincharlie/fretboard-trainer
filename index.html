Welcome to the Fretboard Trainer!

How to play:

  1. When you are ready click the 'Start Game' button below
  2. A note will be highlighted on the guitar fretboard above
  3. Select one of the multi-choice answers which will be displayed
  4. Click the 'Check Answer' button to find out how you did and move on to the next question
  5. You game progress will be displayed on the the right hand side

How to adjust the game:

Use the settings controls on the left hand side to adjust the difficulty of the game.
Simply select 'Apply' to apply your settings or 'Reset Game' to refresh back to the default settings and this instructions page.
(Note: Applying new settings will reset the game and your current progress will be lost)
