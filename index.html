Welcome to the Fretboard Trainer!

How to play:

  1. When you are ready to begin, click the 'START GAME' button below
  2. A note will be highlighted on the guitar fretboard above
  3. Select one of the multi-choice answers which will be displayed
  4. Click the 'CHECK ANSWER' button to find out how if you got it right
  5. Your score will be updated and displayed in the 'Game Progress' section

How to adjust the game:

Use the controls in the 'Game Settings' section to adjust the difficulty of the game.
Simply select 'APPLY & START' to apply your settings or 'RESET GAME' to refresh back to the default settings and this instruction screen.
(Note: Applying any new settings will reset the game and your current progress will be lost)
